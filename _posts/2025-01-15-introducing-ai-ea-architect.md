## What is AI EA Architect?

AI EA Architect is an intelligent platform that brings together:

- **Architecture as Code**: Version-controlled, executable architecture documentation
- **Compliance as Code**: Automated compliance validation embedded in your architecture
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Real-time insights and recommendations

## Key Features

### 1. Automated Documentation
Our AI automatically generates and maintains architecture documentation, keeping it synchronized with your actual systems.

### 2. Continuous Compliance
Real-time monitoring ensures your architecture remains compliant with regulatory frameworks including GDPR, SOC 2, HIPAA, and more.

### 3. Pattern Recognition
The AI learns from industry best practices and your organization's patterns to suggest optimizations.

### 4. Integration Ready
Seamlessly integrates with your existing tools including:
- Cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- CI/CD pipelines
- Architecture tools (ArchiMate, TOGAF)
- Compliance frameworks
